NYC Thanksgiving Cheat Sheet – November 2025

Celebrating my wife’s 50th birthday in style!

Hotel & Parking Info

  • Hotel: The Hotel @ Fifth Avenue, 17 W 32nd St, NYC 10001
  • Garage: 42 W 32nd St (aka 38 W 32nd), near H-Mart & Dr. Lee’s Acupuncture
  • Garage Entry: Right side of 32nd St (one-way eastbound)
  • Walk Time: ~2 minutes to hotel (~443 ft)

Subway Info

  • Nearest Station: 34th St–Herald Square (B, D, F, M, N, Q, R, W)
  • MetroCard: 7-Day Unlimited ($34), available at kiosks
  • Tips: Use Google Maps or MTA app for directions

Macy's Parade Tips (Nov 27)

  • Prime Spot: Hotel is 1 block from parade end at Macy’s Herald Square
  • Viewing Area: 6th Ave, between 34th–38th St
  • Arrive Early: Be there by 6:30 AM

Thanksgiving Day Food

Skip the turkey! Try these open options:

  • Her Name is Han (Korean comfort food)
  • BCD Tofu House, Jongro BBQ, Izakaya Mew, Woorijip Deli
  • El Sazon (Smash burger champion, Chinatown)
  • Nearby pizza: Joe’s Pizza (Broadway & 40th)

Must-Do Attractions

  • Central Park: Zoo, Wollman Rink, playgrounds
  • 9/11 Memorial & fountains
  • Times Square: M&M World, Disney Store
  • Rockefeller Center: Tree, Lego, Nintendo, St. Patrick’s
  • Bryant Park: Winter Village, ice skating
  • Chelsea Market + High Line

Christmas Attractions

  • Rolf’s German Restaurant: epic decor
  • Miracle NYC Holiday Bars (Henrietta Hudson, Sippin’ Santa)
  • Macy’s Santaland: animatronics & elves
  • Hudson Yards Holiday Lights
  • FAO Schwarz (Rockefeller)
  • Grand Central Market + Whisper Gallery

Flexible Itinerary

  • Nov 26 (Wed): Arrive, Koreatown dinner, Times Square stroll
  • Nov 27 (Thu): Parade, brunch, Central Park or Bryant Park, Santaland
  • Nov 28 (Fri): Rockettes, Rockefeller, FAO Schwarz, Grand Central, Hudson Yards
  • Nov 29 (Sat): Check-out, coffee & bagel, depart (garage holds car until 6 PM)

Extras & Hidden Gems

  • Dyker Heights Lights (Brooklyn - far but amazing)
  • Magnolia Bakery (banana pudding)
  • NY Public Library & Lions (photo op)
  • Holiday Window Walk (5th Ave stores)
  • Luggage storage: Vertoe or Schwartz (Penn Station)
